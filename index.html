The perfect pattern-font for risograph and screen printing.

Asistemática* is typographic-pattern-project, it mixes art & craft and font development.

The letters first were cutted and printed in linoleum, then they were organized and digitized into a font family. The linoleum and the digital letters were used to set two different «spe-zines», one hand printed and the order risograph printed.
Asistematica is an Spe-Zine / font / patterns / experimental / :¬] / project.
Video overview

* Asistemática - Unsystematic adj. 1. Not following or conforming to a system. 2.Said of a person: Who acts without rigidity.

I disagree. This project explores the possibilities of the system; this pattern-font becomes a family in the making, in the workshop space, where around the table on Thursdays we would sit to cut the linoleum, to talk, or to share the time.

* Asistemática adj. 1. Que no sigue o que no se ajusta a un sistema. 2. Dicho de una persona: Que procede sin rigidez.

Disiento. Este proyecto explora las posibilidades de sistema, esta pattern-type-font se constituye familia en el hacer, en el espacio del taller donde al rededor de la mesa —los jueves— nos sentábamos a cortar el linóleo, a conversar o a compartir el tiempo.
